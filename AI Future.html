The global artificial intelligence market is expected to be valued at USD 21.46 billion in 2018 and is likely to reach USO 190.61 billion by 2025, at a CAGR of 36.62% during the forecast period. New launches by large firms such as NIVDIA(US), Intel(US), Xilinx (US), Samsung (South Korea), Micron (US), IBM (US), Google (US), Microsoft Corpora ton (US), and Amazon Web Services (US) will help in the growth of the Al market the coming years. The market growth is attributed to the increasing adoption of cloud-based applications and services, and increasing demand for intelligent virtual assistant, the growth of Al in APAC can be attributed to the adoption of Al services in end-user in duties such as manufacturing, healthcare, and automotive in countries such as Japan, China, and South Korea

Artificial intelligence market for hardware to grow at a high CAGR during forecast period The AI market for hardware is expected to grow at a high CAGR during the forecast period. This can be attributed to the increasing need for hardware platforms with high computing power to run various AI software. The presence of major companies that contribute to the AI sector in North America has made the region a major market for hardware related to AI.
